Our Programmes

Physiotherapy and Wellness

Neurological physiotherapy

We provide interventions which assist an individual to regain or maintain their maximum movement and functional independence.

Musculoskeletal & sports physiotherapy

Our priority is to provide a thorough assessment and treatment for your immediate issue along with we also consider potential contributing factors in order to reduce the likelihood of recurrence.

Pediatric physiotherapy

Aim to reduce disability and the need for surgery with a focus on improving function and increasing independence

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Our primary goals are restitution of function, compensation or adaptation to functional losses,and prevention of secondary complications.

Ergonomic, nutrition, and lifestyle guidance.

We provide excellent ergonomic, nutritional, and lifestyle modification guidance to help you better manage and prevent the recurrence of your health condition.

Women's health physiotherapy

Conditions treated by our physiotherapy specialist include:-

a) Dropping of the pelvic organs, including bladder, bowel, rectum, uterus, vagina.

b) Loss of bladder control, stress urinary incontinence or mixed incontinence.

c) Frequent sudden urges to urinate.

d) Loss of bowel control.

e) Pelvic floor muscle weakness, tightness or pain.

f) Breast cancer including lymphedema management.

g) Pain and dysfunction during and following pregnancy.

h) Pre and postpartum low back pain.

i) Dysmenorrhea.

j) Osteoporosis.

Recurrence Management

schedule regular follow up to ensure that your recovery is progressing as planned and is sustained over the expected period.

About Us

We are committed to helping the local community to remain fit and active through our various treatment and wellness programmes. The ethos of us is to aid recovery through physiotherapy and wellness but also to help patients in being able to self-manage the condition. Whether you are a patient or a sportsperson aiming to return to action or someone needing help with relief from pain, our treatment and advice will be tailored to meet your specific physiotherapy and wellness needs.

Following your initial consultation a treatment plan will be discussed and agreed upon, which will include a realistic prognosis and time frame for recovery.

Our aim is to not only treat the symptoms of your condition but to establish the underlying cause and help prevent recurrence.
